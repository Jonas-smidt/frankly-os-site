How to use it

  1. Fill in your details — the card updates live.
  2. Download card (PNG) and save it to your phone’s photos.
  3. At a meeting or event: open the photo, let them scan it. Done.
  4. Tip: set it as a favorite in your photo app so it’s two taps away.
What data is in the QR code?

A standard vCard with your name, title, Frankly as organization, phone, email, website and LinkedIn (if filled in). Everything is generated locally in your browser — nothing is sent anywhere.

The scan doesn’t work?

Make sure the QR fills a good part of the screen and isn’t too dark. If a very long LinkedIn URL makes the code dense, try leaving it out — fewer characters scan faster.
